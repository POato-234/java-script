What is Basketball

Basketball is a popular team sport where two teams try to score points by shooting a ball through a hoop 10 feet high. Players dribble, pass, and defend while aiming to outscore their opponents. Invented in 1891 by Dr. James Naismith, basketball is fast-paced and played worldwide with games typically lasting four quarters
